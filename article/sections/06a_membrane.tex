

\subsection{Numerical verification and mesh convergence of pre-stretched dielectric elastomers: a circular-equivalent membrane benchmark}

The primary technical objectives of this numerical example are fivefold:

\begin{enumerate}
    \item To verify the algorithmic robustness, stability, and quadratic convergence rate of the proposed Total Lagrangian Finite Element (FEM) formulation under coupled thermoelectromechanical loading.
    \item To assess the performance of the calibrated thermo-electro-mechanical Mooney-Rivlin constitutive model under high electrostatic fields and finite strains.
    \item To validate the kinematic consistency of the multi-configuration pre-stretch implementation ($F_p$) and the enforcement of non-linear electro-mechanical boundary conditions.
    \item To benchmark the numerical results (e.g., radial stretch $\lambda_1$ and out-of-plane displacement) against established analytical solutions or experimental data available in the literature.
    \item To perform a comprehensive mesh convergence study to evaluate spatial discretization sensitivity and ensure mesh-independent stress and electric field fields.
\end{enumerate}
